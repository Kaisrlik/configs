vim
-----

## shortcut

* appends to end of the lines
    `<C+v> + $ + A`
* increment/decrement
    `<C+a>`/`<C+x>`
* uppper/lower/revert cases
    `g+U`/`g+u`/`g+~`

*  go to place where the last change is made
    `\` + .`
